Privacy Notice for EnerChill Ads Analytics

Effective date: June 17, 2026

EnerChill Ads Analytics is an internal advertising reporting and optimization recommendation tool. It is designed to read advertising performance data from Amazon Ads and generate reports. It does not collect shoppers' personal information and does not automatically modify advertising campaigns, bids, budgets, prices, coupons, or product listings.

1. Who We Are

EnerChill Ads Analytics is operated by the owner of the EnerChill Amazon advertising account for internal business reporting and advertising optimization analysis.

2. Information We Access

When authorized by the account owner, the tool may access advertising account and campaign performance data through Amazon Ads APIs, including:

  • advertising account and profile identifiers;
  • campaign, ad group, keyword, targeting, and search term data;
  • impressions, clicks, cost, attributed sales, attributed orders, ACOS, ROAS, and related reporting metrics;
  • advertised product identifiers such as SKU, ASIN, campaign names, and ad group names;
  • technical logs needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the reporting workflow.

3. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

The tool is not designed to collect shopper-level personal information. We do not intentionally collect customer names, delivery addresses, phone numbers, payment card information, buyer messages, passwords, verification codes, or other direct shopper personal data.

4. How We Use Information

Purpose Use
Advertising reports Generate weekly and monthly reports about ad spend, sales attribution, search terms, targeting, and campaign performance.
Optimization recommendations Identify negative keyword candidates, bid reduction candidates, budget allocation opportunities, and listing improvement priorities.
Profit analysis Combine advertising cost with locally supplied cost and profit data to estimate net profit and break-even advertising thresholds.
Security and troubleshooting Maintain logs needed to diagnose errors, monitor API jobs, and prevent unauthorized access.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell advertising data or share it with third-party advertisers. Data may be processed by infrastructure or software service providers used to host, secure, or operate the reporting workflow, only as needed for those services.

6. Data Retention

Advertising reports and analysis outputs are retained only for business reporting, performance comparison, audit, and troubleshooting purposes. Data that is no longer needed should be deleted or archived according to internal business requirements.

7. Security

API credentials, access tokens, refresh tokens, passwords, and verification codes must not be published in reports, screenshots, logs, public repositories, or chat messages. Credentials should be stored in secure local environment variables, a credential manager, or another restricted storage mechanism.

8. Automated Actions

The current tool is read-only. It generates recommendations for human review. It does not automatically create, update, pause, or delete Amazon Ads campaigns, keywords, targets, bids, budgets, prices, coupons, or listings.

9. Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice as the tool changes. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when it was last updated.
